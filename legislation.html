Legislation

Stay up to date with the latest legislation and regulations.

The Mine Health and Safety Act (MHSA), Act 29 of 1996 was promulgated following the outcomes of the Leon Commission. Before the promulgation of the MHSA, the safety of the South African mine workers was provided for in the Mines and Works Act No. 12 of 1911 and its applicable regulations.
